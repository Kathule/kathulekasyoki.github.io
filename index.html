power bi

This project involved developing a sales dashboard in Power BI focusing on car sales in Southern Africa. The purpose was to provide stakeholders with a comprehensive overview of sales data, enabling them to make informed decisions efficiently.

The dashboard effectively summarizes sales data by country, dates, revenue, and profits. Additionally, it highlights the top-selling car brands and models, allowing stakeholders to quickly identify trends and patterns in sales performance.

tableau

This project entailed developing a Tableau dashboard utilizing Airbnb data, aimed at aiding investors in making well-informed decisions regarding property investment.

The dashboard highlights various crucial factors for Airbnb investment, encompassing aspects such as property price, size, and location, along with the generated revenue.
